SIDESA - Sistem Informasi Desa

SIDESA - Sistem Informasi Desa is a Java-based mobile application that aims to provide comprehensive and reliable information about a village. The application offers various features, including the village's biography, information about village officials and their roles, geographical location of the village, budget allocation, and the village's potential and resources. With SIDESA, users can easily access and understand essential aspects related to the village, providing opportunities for community members and stakeholders to collaborate effectively in the development and sustainable growth of the village.
